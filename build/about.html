Welcome to Country Quiz

Go To Game

In Country Quiz, you can test your knowledge of world geography by guessing countries based on their outlines.

How to Play

  1. Select the number of countries you want to guess.
  2. Choose a continent or select "All Continents."
  3. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  4. A country outline will appear on the screen.
  5. Guess the country and type your answer in the input field.
  6. If you need help, click "Hint" for a clue.
  7. Click "Submit" to check your answer.

Game Rules

  • You can select between 1 and 100 countries to guess.
  • Choose a continent or select "All Continents" to determine the pool of countries.
  • You have a limited number of hints.
  • Correct answers earn you points.
  • Try to achieve the highest score and become a geography master!
